📋 Report Methodology — GKT Campaign Attribution (March 2026 · April 2026)

Data sources: PostHog (signup events) + HubSpot (contact creation, deals, original source). No other sources are used.

1. Signup Source (PostHog)

Event: $identify · Period: 2026-03-01 ≤ timestamp < 2026-04-01 · Filters: workspace_id IS NOT NULL (confirms real B2B signup) · email IS NOT NULL · Exclude bots matching geogatedctr%@gmail.com

2. Campaign Attribution

Field used: person.properties.$initial_utm_campaign (Initial UTM — NOT session UTM). Only the 3 exact campaign values are tracked:
GKT-BOFU-META-CV-US-B2C-AON · GKT-BOFU-GG-CV-US-B2C-DG-AON · GKT-BOFU-GG-CV-US-B2B-sembrand-AON
If a contact appears multiple times, records are grouped by email + campaign and the earliest min(timestamp) is used as the signup date.

3. New vs. Retargeting Classification (HubSpot)

Contact email from PostHog is matched to HubSpot by email field.
New = hs_createdate ≥ 2026-03-01 (first time in HubSpot) · Retargeting = hs_createdate < 2026-03-01 (pre-existing contact re-engaged by campaign)

4. Original Source (informational only)

Field: hs_analytics_source from HubSpot. Shows how the contact was originally acquired — not how they were attributed to this campaign. This field is purely descriptive and does not affect inclusion logic.

5. Revenue Classification (HubSpot deals)

Only deals with createdate ≥ 2026-03-01 are counted.
Closed Won (CW) = hs_is_closed_won = true · Under Construction (UC) = active deal (not closed) · No Job = no March HubSpot deal found.
Without GoWork integration, Renewal classification is not available — contacts should be validated line by line in HubSpot.

6. Week Definition

W1 = Mar 1–8 · W2 = Mar 9–15 · W3 = Mar 16–22 · W4 = Mar 23–31 · Based on PostHog signup date (cs field), not HubSpot createdate.

7. Confidentiality — GKT Protocol

Contact emails are not displayed in the detail tables. Each row uses a sequential Contact ID (C-001, C-002, …). Raw data with emails is available upon request to the GKT team lead.
